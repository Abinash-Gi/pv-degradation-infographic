Key Challenges & Future Outlook

Long-Term Stability

Perovskite materials remain susceptible to degradation from heat, humidity, and UV radiation, posing a challenge for the 25-30 year lifespan expected of commercial modules.

Manufacturing Scalability

Transitioning from lab-scale spin-coating to industrial methods like blade-coating while maintaining film uniformity and quality over large areas is a major hurdle.

Standardized Testing

Inconsistent testing criteria and a need for real-world performance validation beyond AM1.5G conditions limit direct comparisons and deployment readiness.
